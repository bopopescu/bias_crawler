sentiment of text percentages:

Using Naive Bayes Algorithim (an algorithim that uses machine learning to determine the sentiment of text) the sentece around each link has been classified as either positive, negative, or neutral.

Depending on this result, the 'calculated bias' is adjusted.

For example: if a link, going to a fox news article has a negativly sentimented sentence around it, the score with be pushed slighty in the liberal direction.
